YouTube Thumbnail Grabber is a free online media application which allows you to view and download any Youtube thumbnail preview image.
Currently supported formats: YouTube (MQ, HD, HQ, SD, 1080p, 4K), This free and fast thumbnail grabber allows you to save any YouTube thumbnail on your computer, smartphone or nearly any other device for future use.

How to grab and download a YouTube thumbnail preview image:
1. Paste your YouTube URL in the box and press Get Thumbnail (or Enter).
2. Click on the resolution you want.
3. Right-click the image and select "Save image as..."


Notice: some links do not have max resolution versions (Even if the video is in 1080p or higher resolution!)
